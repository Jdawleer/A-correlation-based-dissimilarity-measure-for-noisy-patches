Here are some preliminary results of our method on the lena image. For the local methods, we set a size of patches s=10*10 with an overlap of 2 (we take half of the patches), unless it is particularly said otherwise. The optimization is done on 20 iterations for computational time purposes.

The best results are obtained for the Residu-1-TV method with lambda=60. But the improvements from the MAP-TV method is not significant yet. Indeed, the SNR is only slightly higher and the Residu-1-TV results present some disturbing artifacts. However, we can notice that the method successfully retrieves some textures.
Comparing the global and local methods, the performances improve drastically. The global methods can't retrieve the texture and let through a lot of noise.
